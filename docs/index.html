ACME SAFARI – is a nature reserve company, specializing in combining your dream African nature tour experience with world class tour guides.

We offer luxury accommodation at the most distinguished Lodges in Africa, paired with musical entertainment from some of the world’s top professional musicians. Our lodges are selected with privacy, luxury, relaxation and pristine scenic beauty in mind, and our tour guides are hand picked, based on their extensive careers as professionals with the knowledge to give you everything you need to know about the Safari.

Beuatiful Scenery

Experience the beautiful lands within the safari and enjoy because around the best that nature has to offer.

Musical Entertainment

On this journey you will experience a wide range of musical talent from world class musicians all over the world and you will also get exposed to young African talent.

World Class Tour Guides

Have no fear when you are travelling with our extensively experienced tour guides that are going to show you around the Safari. They have in-depth knowledge of the Safari and are trained professionals that can help you with their needs.

Beautiful Lodges

Sleep in a nice comfortable bed surrounded by the sounds of nature and away from city noises where you can fall asleep in peace. 5 star rated accommodations with entertainment system you are probably not going to use since the beautiful outdoors would be calling you.
